Project Spotlight: Web Development

Building custom and reliable websites for local businesses.

Client-Focused Design for a Local Photographer (2024)

This project involved creating a professional portfolio website for a local photographer. The primary focus was on client collaboration and delivering a site that met their specific aesthetic and functional needs within a strict timeframe.

Built on the Wordpress framework, the final product was a six-page site designed to elegantly display the photographer's work. The process involved regular meetings with the client to understand their vision, provide updates, and ensure all requirements were met. It was a valuable experience in project management and client communication.

Custom Website for a Local Swimming School (2022)

I was approached by a local swimming school to develop a ten-page website from scratch. The project required a combination of front-end design and ensuring all content was accurate and catered to the client's needs.

Using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I built a user-friendly and reliable website. A key feature was a functional contact form for client inquiries. This project was a fantastic opportunity to apply my coding skills to a real-world problem and focus on delivering a dependable final product that met the client's expectations.
